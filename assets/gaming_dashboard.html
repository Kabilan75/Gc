Key conclusion for SideFest

UK is world-class in creative skills — Storytelling, Unreal, Maya, C++. These skills open doors in France, Japan, USA and Sweden whose skill profiles match UK most closely. Communication is #1 globally and in the UK — teaching it prepares young people for careers worldwide, not just locally.
